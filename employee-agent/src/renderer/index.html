Privacy & Consent

This application will track:

  • Mouse event counts (not positions)
  • Keyboard event counts (not actual keystrokes)
  • Active application names
  • Idle/active time

What we DO NOT track:

  • Screenshots or screen content
  • Actual keystroke content
  • Personal data or files
  • Activity outside work hours

Tracking begins only after you click "Start Day" and stops immediately when you click "End Day" or close this application.
